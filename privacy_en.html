Privacy Policy

Last updated: 02.08.2024

Welcome to PressReader. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the application depends on the content and materials you use, and includes:

  • Personal Data: If you register to the app, we collect personal information such as your email address, or social login information provided by Google or Apple (including your name and profile picture).
  • Followed Sources and Bookmarks: We collect the sources you follow, any sources you manually add, and bookmarks you save on your account.
  • Usage Data: We use Firebase Analytics and Google Analytics to track user behavior within the app.
  • Crash Reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports to improve the app’s stability.
  • Advertising Data: We use AdMob to serve ads to non-subscribed users and may collect data related to ad interactions.
  • Subscription Data: We use RevenueCat to manage and track your subscription status and activity within the app. This includes subscription tier and billing information.

How We Use Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the application to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Provide user support.
  • Analyze usage and improve the app.
  • Track and manage crash reports.
  • Enable user-to-user communications.
  • Serve ads and measure ad performance.
  • Manage and track subscriptions.
  • Manage followed and manually added sources for personalized content.

Please be aware that the data displayed in the app comes directly from these sources and is subject to their own privacy practices. PressReader does not control or guarantee the accuracy, timeliness, or completeness of this data.

  • Business Transfers: We may share or transfer your information in connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business to another company.

Third-Party Services

PressReader uses the following third-party services:

We retain your data for as long as you maintain an active account. If you delete your account, we will remove all associated data from our systems within 30 days.

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. Your data is securely stored on Supabase, and sensitive information and tokens are stored in the iOS keychain.

Children’s Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information from our servers immediately.
